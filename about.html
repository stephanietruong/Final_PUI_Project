Here is a little more about me.

Hi, I'm Stephanie, a New Yorker who is passionate in designing and creating new innovative experiences and products. I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University where I was double majoring in Human Computer Interaction and Business Administration with a minor in Media Design. I have experience in UI/UX design, service design, and programming.

I want to create products and services that provide meaningful experiences to people. I enjoy talking to users, customers, and key stakeholders to understand their painpoints and utilizing these insights to create solutions that will actually benefit the users. I am interested in pairing data analytics with design to uncover new opportunities for innovation. I want to bring the empathy to the data and use it to help tell a story.

During my free time, I love to explore new places. Normally, you can find me at any coffee shop, working and sipping on a hot cup of latte. Therefore, feel free to connect with me and we can go get coffee together!
