About

Hebo

A personal post-operative companion and assistant

Patients recovering from skin cancer surgery often did not completely understand and had numerous concerns about their post-operative care, which resulted in clinicians receiving a high volume of redundant and low-priority phone calls. Our team was tasked with developing a post-operative care assistant that could handle these concerns and acted as a constant companion throughout a patient’s recovery. We conducted numerous stages of research and prototyping to finally create Hebo, an app- based chatbot that used voice, text, and images to convey care information.

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Category: Chatbot

TrustyTech

A mobile app to optimize appointments with home service providers

Many customers are dissatisfied with the service scheduling and delivery experiences with HVAC service providers. With TrustyTech, customers will no longer have to worry about being at home during long appointment windows, and technicians are able to efficiently add more jobs throughout the day. TrustyTech is a mobile application that aims to build trust between home service providers and their customers, in order to increase the comfort of unsupervised appointments. This allows both parties to be more in control of their schedules.

  • Duration: 4 weeks
  • Category: Mobile App

Control Redesign

Redesign the controls for window blinds

Controls had been the fundamental building blocks of interaction design to help relay the user's intention or desire for the system to understand and act on the user’s intent. The goal of the project was to find a real-world control that could be improve and re-designed the control. For this project, I chose to redesign the controls for window blinds. With the new design, users would now be able to move the blinds up and down as well as change the angle of the slats all in one control. This new control made it easier for users to quickly adjust the blind without dealing with repetitive motion of a button or the hassle of pulling on a cord or turning a rod.

  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Category: 3D Prototyping

Biodiversity Dashboard

Create a single screen dashboard on the effects of climate change

The goal of this project was to design a single screen dashboard to show the critical effects of climate change and biodiversity on Sub-Saharan Africa. Our client for this project was an advisory board of scientists who wanted to stress the important results of their research on climate change and biodiversity and the monitoring efforts that should be acted upon. Their primary goal was to urge the policy makers to take appropriate action to mitigate the effect that climate change have on biodiversity before it’s too late. Our users, on the other hand, were national-level policy makers who were swamped with a lot of information and distraction. They did not have a lot of time to dedicate time to focus on the dashboard. Therefore, as designers, we must create a board to satisfy both our clients’ and user’s individual goals.

  • Duration: 4 weeks
  • Category: Data Visualization

Retail Fitting Room

A conversational user interface to enhance the retail experience


The goal of the project was to create a novel conversational user interface (CUI) to improve the retail store dressing room experiences. A conversation user interface was a platform that mimicked a conversation with a real human. Some examples of CUIs were chatbots or voice assistants such as Google Home or Amazon Echo. For our CUI, we designed a fitting room personal assistant that helped customers shop for jeans. The CUI would walk the user through steps to make sure their jeans fit and it also guided them through a series of small exercises to test out the jeans.

  • Duration: 3 weeks
  • Category: Experience Prototype
